01 — what this file teaches

Blind means blind until the last vote.

This is the pattern agents get wrong: they reveal each case as soon as it is voted. Don't. While a single vote is missing, the page shows only progress and your own pick — no identity, no numbers, no partial tally.

When the last vote lands, everything opens at once and the closing block appears at the end of the page: champion, summed leaderboard and a winner-vs-your-pick comparison. That closing block is the payoff for finishing. Full reference in references/polls.md.
